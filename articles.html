Applications of Machine Learning

Machine learning (ML) is a crucial subset of artificial intelligence (AI) that enables computers to learn from data and make decisions without explicit programming. Rooted in the 1950s with significant milestones such as the development of neural networks, ML has revolutionized various fields through supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning. Key algorithms include linear regression, decision trees, support vector machines, and neural networks. ML’s applications span from image and speech recognition to predictive analytics and recommendation systems. Despite its transformative potential, challenges like data quality, model interpretability, and overfitting remain. As technology advances, ML is poised to further impact industries and daily life.
